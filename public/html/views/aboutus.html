About Us

Bread of the Mighty Food Bank is a Partner Distribution Organization under Feeding America. Our 20,000 square foot warehouse has 2,600 square feet of cooler and freezer capacity. Thanks to our generous donors, we now have a fleet of 11 trucks and vans. We're on the road five days a week picking up donated food from generous retailers and wholesalers that in turn gets distributed back to the community.

Bread of the Mighty Food Bank serves a five county service area; Alachua, Dixie, Gilchrist, Lafayette and Levy. In an effort to keep perishable and fresh items from sitting in our warehouse, our "Food on the Move" program was established. This ensures fresh & perishable items don't sit in the warehouse and quickly gets into the hands of those in need.
